What is Codebell?

Codebell is a cloud-based QR video intercom system. It allows secure and private communication between two people.
Codebell is easy to set up and doesn't require any hardware or app downloads.
It offers visitor self-help and live assistance without the need for an app.

How Does Codebell Work?

Every access point of Codebell has a unique QR code.
Visitors can scan the QR code to initiate communication with the resident.
The resident can communicate with the visitor in real-time via voice, video, or text.
Codebell allows visitors to leave messages and screenshots, which are saved in the visitor log.
Residents can also use Codebell to call back missed visitors and forward calls to family members.
