How To Use Codebell To Secure Your Home

In this blog, we will guide you on how to use Codebell to secure your home and protect your family.

As a homeowner, ensuring the security of your home and loved ones is a top priority. With the rise of technology, there are now more options available than ever to improve the security of your home. One of the latest and most innovative solutions is the Codebell QR video intercom system.

Step 1: Install Codebell

The first step to using Codebell to secure your home is to install the device. Codebell is incredibly easy to install, and you don't need any technical expertise to set it up. The Codebell device is designed to be placed anywhere in your home, and you don't need to run any wires or hire an installer to install it.


To install Codebell, simply follow these steps:

  1. Download the Codebell app on your smartphone. The app is available on both Android and iOS platforms.
  2. Once the app is installed, create an account with Codebell and log in.
  3. Follow the instructions on the app to pair your smartphone with the Codebell device. This is done by scanning the QR code on the device using the app.
  4. Once the device is paired, you are ready to start using Codebell to secure your home.

Step 2: Set up notifications

The next step is to set up notifications on the Codebell app. Notifications are an essential feature of Codebell as they inform you whenever someone rings the bell or when there is motion detected by the device. Setting up notifications ensures that you are always aware of what is happening at your doorstep, even when you are not home.


To set up notifications, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Codebell app on your smartphone.
  2. Click on the Settings icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  3. Select Notifications from the menu.
  4. Toggle on the notifications you want to receive, such as motion detection, doorbell ring, or missed calls.

Step 3: Connect with visitors

One of the most significant benefits of Codebell is that it allows you to connect with visitors to your home, even when you are not there. When someone rings the bell or when there is motion detected by the device, you will receive a notification on your smartphone. You can then open the app and connect with the visitor through a video call.


To connect with visitors, follow these steps:

  1. When you receive a notification, open the Codebell app on your smartphone.
  2. Click on the video call button to start a video call with the visitor.
  3. Once you have connected with the visitor, you can talk to them and see them through the video call.
  4. If you are not available to talk, you can let the visitor know that you are unavailable through the app.

Step 4: Manage access

Codebell also allows you to manage access to your home by granting or denying entry to visitors. With Codebell, you can create virtual keys for your family members and trusted friends, which they can use to enter your home. You can also revoke access to these virtual keys at any time.


To manage access, follow these steps:

  1. Open the Codebell app on your smartphone.
  2. Click on the Settings icon in the bottom right corner of the screen.
  3. Select Access from the menu.
  4. Add the email address or phone number of the person you want to grant access to.
  5. Choose the access permissions you want to grant to the person.
  6. Click Save.

Step 5: Monitor your home

Codebell is also an excellent tool for monitoring your home, especially when you are not there. With the motion detection feature, you can receive alerts on your smartphone whenever there is any movement detected by the device. This can help you keep an eye on your home even when you're away on vacation or at work. You can also check the live stream anytime to see what's happening in and around your home.

Another way to monitor your home with Codebell is to set up a schedule. You can set the device to automatically turn on and off at specific times of the day. This feature is great for homeowners who want to keep an eye on their home during certain hours of the day or night.

5. Use the Two-Way Audio Feature

Codebell also features a two-way audio feature that allows you to communicate with anyone at your door even if you are not physically present. This feature is particularly useful for homeowners who want to speak to delivery drivers or visitors without having to open the door.

The two-way audio feature also enables you to scare off potential intruders. If you notice someone suspicious lurking around your home, you can use the audio feature to warn them off and let them know that you are watching.

6. Protect Your Privacy

While Codebell is an excellent tool for securing your home, it's important to remember that it also has the potential to invade your privacy. To prevent unauthorized access to your device, it's essential to set a strong password and enable two-factor authentication.

It's also important to be mindful of where you place your Codebell device. You should avoid placing it in areas where it can capture sensitive information such as credit card details or personal information. You should also ensure that you are not recording audio or video in places where people have a reasonable expectation of privacy, such as bathrooms or bedrooms.

7. Regularly Update Your Device

To ensure that your Codebell device is always up-to-date and secure, it's important to regularly update its software. Codebell provides regular updates that fix bugs, enhance performance, and add new features to the device.

To update your Codebell device, simply log in to the app, navigate to the settings menu, and select "firmware update." The app will then guide you through the process of updating your device.

Conclusion

Codebell is an excellent tool for securing your home. With its easy-to-use interface, customizable settings, and powerful features, it's an excellent choice for homeowners who want to protect their property and loved ones. By following the tips in this guide, you can ensure that your Codebell device is set up correctly and used effectively to provide the maximum level of security and convenience for your home.
