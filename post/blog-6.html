What is Codebell QR Video Intercom?

Codebell QR Video Intercom is a cutting-edge security solution that combines modern technology with traditional intercom systems. It uses QR code technology to enable residents to enter their property easily and quickly. The intercom system is equipped with a mobile camera and a microphone, enabling residents to communicate with visitors and grant access remotely. This technology allows residents to monitor their property and visitors in real-time, providing a secure and convenient access control solution.

How Does Codebell QR Video Intercom Work?

The Codebell QR Video Intercom is a wireless system that connects to a secure online platform. When a visitor arrives at the property, they scan the QR code using their smartphone, which sends a request to the resident's Codebell app. The resident can then see and speak to the visitor through the app, granting or denying access as required. The intercom system can be accessed remotely from anywhere with an internet connection, giving residents the flexibility and convenience to manage their property's security from anywhere.
