What is Codebell and what does it do?

Codebell is a QR video intercom technology that enables secure and private communication between two users. It offers a great option for businesses and individuals who value their privacy while staying connected.

What is the residential use case for Codebell?

In residential use cases, Codebell serves as an outdoor video intercom system that allows homeowners to identify visitors and decide whether to grant them access.

Can Codebell be used in cars?

Yes, Codebell can be installed on car windshields to facilitate communication between drivers, parking attendants, and security personnel.

What is the commercial use case for Codebell?

In commercial use cases, Codebell can be used in offices and shops to connect customers with representatives who can assist them even when the office is closed. It also serves as a seamless communication channel between guests and staff in public places such as hotels and airports.

What is QR Video Intercom and how does it work?

QR Video Intercom is a cloud-based communication system that allows users to manage every point of every facility with ease. Users simply place their QR code anywhere for unparalleled communication and support. It enables users to connect with representatives for assistance and support, making it a powerful tool for businesses and individuals who value efficient and personalized communication.

What are the benefits of using Codebell for communication?

Codebell offers secure and private communication between two users, making it an ideal option for those who value their privacy. It also provides seamless communication between visitors and staff in public places, and between customers and representatives in offices and shops. In residential settings, it enables homeowners to identify visitors and control access to their property.

Is Codebell easy to use?

Yes, Codebell is easy to use. It uses QR codes for communication, which are simple to scan using a smartphone camera. The cloud-based communication system is also user-friendly, providing complete control over every point of every facility.

Can Codebell be customized to suit individual needs?

Yes, Codebell can be customized to suit individual needs. It offers a range of features that can be tailored to meet the specific communication requirements of businesses and individuals.

Does Codebell require any special hardware or equipment to work?

No, Codebell does not require any special hardware or equipment to work. The cloud-based communication system can be accessed from any device with an internet connection. No hardware, Nothing to install, No app download(for visitor), Built on the cloud, Just a QR code.
